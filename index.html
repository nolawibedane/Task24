Is This The Year Manchester City Win The Treble?

The Treble is considered one of the greatest compilation of accomplishments in English football. The Treble consists of the Premier League, FA Cup & the renowned Champions League. Winning anyone one of these competitions is no easy feat let alone winning all three of them in a single season. The first and last team to ever win all three of these competitions was the 1998/99 squad, Manchester United led by one of the greatest football managers of all time Alex Ferguson. But this blog isn't about them, rather we're here to talk about Manchester City's chances of taking it all home. Manchester City are no strangers to winning, ever since the day Pep Guardiola joined the team as manager in 2016 all the team has done is win. 4 Premier League titles and 1 FA Cup title. Yet one title keeps eluding the highly regarded team and that is the Champions League. We'll be discussing the past misteps of the team and how the future may look bright all on this blog
