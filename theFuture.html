Why Is This THE YEAR?

  • Overall Perfomance of The Team
  • Pep's Concentration
  • A New Striker




The team has been playing some of the best football it ever has been. Besting some of the best teams in dominant fashion and never dropping a game to 2nd place team in the Premier League, Arsenal. Many have seem to attribute this to Pep Guardiola's reignited passion for the team with never before seen tactics being sprung into play and alot more envolvement into the team. But obviously we can not talk about Manchester City's future and current success without bringing up the blossoming Star Erling Halaand. A player who at his first year playing for the Premier League has already broken the record for most goals in all competitions not to mention he is only 22 years old, he makes it look all too easy. Ever since Manchester City said bye to their franchise player Kun Aguero there has been a wide gap in the Striker position and Erling Halaand makes sure he feels that gap and more. These are all the reasons I believe that this year is the year Manchester City finally grab the Champions League trophy that they've been missing.
